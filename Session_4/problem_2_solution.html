Problem 2

You run a catering business for the local zoo, and you need to figure out how much food to order and what the cost is going to be. Each animal has its own list of preferred foods, and each food item has a cost.

You're going to be filling in code inside the catering() function. The function takes in two arguments: a DICTIONARY of animals and their food preferences (which are stored as ARRAYS), and a second DICTIONARY of foods and their prices. The function should return the total cost of all the food you need to order for the animals in the zoo.

Example

Dictionary of animals and their food preferences: animals = {"Gorilla" : ["Cheeseburger"], "Giraffe" : ["Boba", "Salad"], "Lion" : ["Steak", "Cheeseburger"]}
Dictionary of foods and their prices: foods = {"Cheeseburger" : 6, "Steak" : 14, "Boba" : 4, "Salad" : 5, "Milkshake" : 4}

Calling the catering function: catering(animals, foods) --> 35
